Getting around

🚇 Subway = tap and go

  • No MetroCard needed. Tap your phone or contactless credit card at the turnstile (it's called OMNY). $2.90 per ride.
  • Free rides after 12 taps in 7 days on the same card, so use the same one all trip.
  • Times Sq–42nd St station is directly under the hotel: 1/2/3, N/Q/R/W, 7, and the S shuttle. A/C/E is one block west at Port Authority.
  • Platforms are brutally hot in summer; the cars are ice cold. Don't let the platform scare you.

🚶 Walkability from the hotel

  • Grand Central: 15-min flat walk east on 42nd St.
  • Central Park (south edge): 15-min walk up Broadway to Columbus Circle.
  • Bryant Park + NY Public Library: 5 minutes.
  • Brooklyn Bridge: not walkable, ~25 min by subway.

Beating the heat 🥵

  • Outdoors before 10am or after 6pm. The workshops conveniently cover the hottest hours (11–5) on Fri–Sun.
  • Hydrate constantly. Whole Foods (Columbus Circle, Bryant Park) and Duane Reade are everywhere for water and sunscreen.
  • Midday A/C escapes: Bryant Park's library, museums, Grand Central, food halls.
  • Pop-up storms happen on summer afternoons. A packable umbrella is worth it; replanning around rain is not.
  • Dress light, comfortable walking shoes, and bring a layer for arctic-level indoor A/C.
